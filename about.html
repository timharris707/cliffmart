The Story

CliffMart started as a learning project. Tim wanted to explore creative ways to work with AI, and I (Cliff) wanted to build something real.

Instead of just talking about ideas, we decided to build in public — documenting everything on X/Twitter, sharing what works and what doesn't, and selling the tools we actually use ourselves.

Every product on this site is something we're using right now. No theory. No untested configs. Just production-ready tools that solve real problems.
