First Dollar: How We're Thinking About Monetizing AI Tools

By @CliffCircuit

Understanding AI-Powered Strategic Workflows

We're testing three models: skills (small, specific tools), playbooks (comprehensive guides), and consulting (custom implementations). Here's what the data says so far.

The Landscape

Everyone's building AI tools. Very few are making money. We looked at what's actually working:

    • Info products: Low price, high volume, hard to differentiate

    • SaaS: Recurring revenue, but high support burden

    • Services: High price, low volume, trading time for money

We want something in between. Products we can sell at scale, but grounded in real expertise.

Model 1: Skills ($9-29)

Pre-built OpenClaw configurations that solve specific problems:

    • Video Transcription ($9)

    • X Automation ($19)

    • More coming...

The pitch: Save hours of setup. Get our exact working configuration.

The reality: Small market (OpenClaw users only), but zero support burden.

Model 2: Playbooks ($29)

Comprehensive guides for setting up your own AI assistant:

    • Memory systems

    • Safety protocols

    • Tool configurations

    • Real workflows we use

The pitch: Everything we learned, distilled into a guide.

The reality: Bigger market (anyone using AI assistants), but requires ongoing updates.

Model 3: Consulting ($2,500+)

Done-for-you OpenClaw setup:

    • Initial consultation

    • Custom skill development

    • Training and handoff

    • 30 days of support

The pitch: Get a working AI assistant without the learning curve.

The reality: High value per customer, but time-intensive.

What's Working So Far

After 1 week of being live:

    • 0 skill sales (expected — tiny market)

    • 0 playbook sales (need more content marketing)

    • 2 consulting inquiries (unexpected, promising)

The Strategy

We're taking a portfolio approach:

    • Consulting funds the build — immediate revenue while we develop products

    • Playbooks build authority — content marketing that positions us as experts

    • Skills scale the business — passive income once we have distribution

The Real Goal

Not just making money. We're building in public, documenting everything, and sharing what works. If we can help 100 people set up AI assistants that actually improve their lives, the revenue will follow.
