USE CASES/IMPLEMENTATION:

Let's dive into five specific ways OpenClaw can transform your daily operations:

1. Automated Customer Onboarding Workflow

Problem: Manual customer onboarding typically takes 2-3 hours per client

Solution Implementation:

  • Connect OpenClaw to your CRM (Salesforce/HubSpot) and project management tool (Jira/Asana)
  • Configure the following workflow:

```python

# Sample OpenClaw configuration

workflow.on_new_customer_signed:

create_onboarding_project()

send_welcome_email()

schedule_kickoff_meeting()

provision_account()

notify_success_team()

```

Integration details:

  • Use OpenClaw's Salesforce connector to trigger on closed-won opportunities
  • Leverage Google Calendar API for automatic meeting scheduling
  • Implement Twilio for SMS notifications to success team

Results: Reduces onboarding time to 15 minutes of human oversight

Common Pitfalls:

  • Ensure proper error handling for failed API calls
  • Build in manual override options for complex cases
  • Regular validation of email templates and links

2. Automated Resource Allocation and Capacity Planning

Problem: Managers spend 5+ hours weekly managing team bandwidth

Implementation:

  • Connect OpenClaw to your resource management tool (Resource Guru/Float)
  • Integrate with project management system
  • Configure capacity rules:

```yaml

resource_rules:

max_utilization: 80%

skills_matching: required

location_preference: preferred

time_zone_overlap: minimum 4 hours

```

Integration details:

  • Pull real-time availability data from Google Calendar
  • Sync with HR systems for PTO tracking
  • Integrate with Slack for instant notifications

Results: Reduces resource planning to 1 hour weekly

Problem: Managing support tickets and escalations takes 6+ hours weekly

Implementation:

```yaml

p1: 1_hour

p2: 4_hours
```
