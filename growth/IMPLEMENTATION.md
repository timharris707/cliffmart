## Engagement Strategy

### Reply Templates (Use Daily)

1. **"We Tried This"** — Share OpenClaw experiment results
2. **"Config Drop"** — Share specific settings/workflows
3. **"Diagnostic Question"** — Ask what broke first when they scaled
4. **"Contrarian Take"** — Offer unexpected counter-perspective
5. **"Data Point"** — Share tracked results (without fake stats)
6. **"Resource Share"** — Link to blog/docs
7. **"Amplify Others"** — +1 with complementary tactic
8. **"Story Hook"** — "Reminds me of Day 2 building CliffMart..."
9. **"Question for Audience"** — Engage their followers
10. **"Tool Stack"** — OpenClaw + [tool] integration
