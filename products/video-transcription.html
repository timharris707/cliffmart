How It Works

Transcribe a video bash 
# YouTube video
video-transcription https://youtube.com/watch?v=...

# TikTok video
video-transcription https://tiktok.com/@user/video/...

# X/Twitter video
video-transcription https://x.com/user/status/...

# Output saved to:
# ~/transcripts/2026-02-24-143052-youtube-VIDEO_ID.txt
