## Key Findings

**Average: ~3,200-3,300 words per blog post**

**Range:**
- Shortest: Painters (~2,300 words)
- Longest: Dance Studios (~3,655 words)
- Most common range: 3,000-3,600 words

**Pattern:** Felix's articles are consistently long-form, detailed guides that follow a similar structure:
1. Problem identification (the pain point)
2. Cost of inaction (revenue loss stats)
3. OpenClaw solution overview
4. Multiple specific use cases/agents (usually 4-6)
5. Implementation steps
6. ROI calculations
7. Call to action

This length allows for:
- Deep problem exploration
- Multiple specific examples
- Concrete implementation steps
- Data/stats to support claims
- SEO optimization (long-tail keywords)

## Recommendation for CliffMart Blog

Target: **3,000-3,500 words per post**

This length hits the sweet spot for:
- SEO (Google favors comprehensive content)
- Reader value (substantial, actionable content)
- Shareability (long enough to be "definitive")
- Time investment (readable in 12-15 minutes)
